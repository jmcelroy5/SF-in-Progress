SF IN PROGRESS

growing together

San Fransico is going through an acute

housing shortage

In 1940, the average price

of a home in California

was 20% higher than the US average.

Today, it is 150% higher.

Last year, San Francisco allowed

193 new homes to be built

for every 1,000 new residents.

The Bay Area is growing

And suburban sprawl costs the US $1 trillion a year.

We can no longer afford to build OUT

but need to build IN

We need to build smarter.

Around transit hubs.

And inside city limits.
